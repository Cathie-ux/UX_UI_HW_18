Cathie Yun is an UX/UI designer and art director with extensive experience in solving design problems. She has worked in various industries from wellness, beauty, fashion, food, travel, home, celebrity, lifestyle and educational content for digital and print.


A graduate of The Cooper Union for the Advancement of Science she studied graphic design, calligraphy and photography. While attending The Cooper Union, she worked at The Herb Lubalin Study Center of Design and Typography. Assisting in curating exhibitions, the preservation of design history and archiving of design ephemera. She continues to learn and develop her professional development skills, has a UXUI certification from the General Assembly and Columbia Engineering Bootcamp.
